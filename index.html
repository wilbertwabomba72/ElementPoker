Element Poker

Master Metal, Wood, Water, Fire, and Earth in a tactical single-player card adventure filled with heroes, skill cards, and powerful bosses.

Win With Knowledge

Every duel combines elemental advantage, card rank, health, shields, and hand management. Choose one card in secret, reveal it with your opponent, and turn the five-element cycle into victory.

Three Heroes

Lead the durable Warrior, the focused Ranger, or the flexible Mage. Each hero changes how you draw, defend, and plan your winning rounds.

Fifty Levels

Travel from the Whispering Forest to the Eternal Citadel, earn stars, unlock new challenges, and confront an elemental boss every ten levels.

Offline Adventure

Core gameplay works without a network connection. Progress, hero selection, music, and vibration preferences are stored locally on your device.

Build Your Tactics

Use Shield, Heal, Draw, and Damage skill cards at the right moment. Preserve valuable cards between rounds, adapt to changing hand limits, and learn each boss's special power.
